# About Me!

I am a PhD candidate in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Toronto, with a focus on Computer Vision and Machine Learning. My current research is centered on improving health monitoring systems, particularly in the context of gait analysis and fall risk prediction for older adults with dementia.

## Education

- **Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence, University of Toronto (2021 - Present)**
- **M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence, Ferdowsi University of Mashhad (2015 - 2018)**
- **B.Sc. in Computer Engineering, Ferdowsi University of Mashhad (2010 - 2014)**

## Projects

- **[AMBIENT Project](https://github.com/vida-adeli/AMBIENT-Project)**: Using computer vision technology to analyze gait and predict falls risk in older adults with dementia.
- **Human Pose Forecasting**: Collaboration with Stanford University on human pose and motion prediction.
- **Stock Market Analysis**: NLP-based performance and risk analysis of stock market trends.
